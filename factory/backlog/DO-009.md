id: DO-009
title: Preserve outline scroll position after section rearrange
status: fixed
priority: high
stage: impl
files:
  - src/components/outlineWindow.ts
---

After drag-drop reorder or horizontal level change, the outline modal scrolls back to the top. This happens because `editor.setValue()` triggers Obsidian to re-parse headings, which calls `update()`, which rebuilds the `ul` contents - resetting the content container's `scrollTop`.

Fix: in `update()`, save the content container's `scrollTop` before rebuilding the heading list and restore it after appending the new fragment.

In the `update()` method, right before `ulElement.empty()` (the heading rebuild path), capture `const contentEl = this._containerEl.querySelector(".dynamic-outline-content-container"); const savedScroll = contentEl?.scrollTop ?? 0;`. After `ulElement.appendChild(fragment)`, restore it with `if (contentEl) contentEl.scrollTop = savedScroll;`.
