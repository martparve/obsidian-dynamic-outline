id: DO-006
title: Fix collapse icons broken by draggable attribute
status: fixed
priority: high
stage: impl
files:
  - src/components/outlineWindow.ts
---

The `draggable="true"` on li elements causes the browser to intercept mousedown on the collapse icon as a drag start, preventing the click event from firing.

Fix: in `_onDragStart`, check if `event.target` is inside a `.dynamic-outline-collapse-icon` and call `event.preventDefault(); return;` to let the click through instead.
