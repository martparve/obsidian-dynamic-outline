title: Fix grab cursor not showing on heading text
status: fixed
priority: high
stage: impl
files:
  - styles.css
---

The `cursor: grab` on `#dynamic-outline li` is overridden by `cursor: default` on `#dynamic-outline li a`. Change `#dynamic-outline li a` to `cursor: grab` and add `cursor: grabbing` for `#dynamic-outline li.dragging a`.
